About Us


We are a startup named Disposable Marketing. Our company is aimed at printing advertisements on disposable tea, juice, and other cups. We accept all kinds of advertisements — including educational institute promotions, restaurant ads, political campaigns, events, and movie promotions.

We don’t just print and sit. We actively distribute these printed cups to various shops and places where the advertisement will have the most impact. From student areas and business hubs to political rally spots and entertainment venues, our aim is to reach the right audience at the right time — with the cup in their hand.

Our Services


Educational Institutes

We design and print advertisements for coaching centers, schools, and colleges, and distribute them near student hubs, libraries, and academic zones.

Restaurants & Cafes

We help restaurants and cafes attract more customers by printing their menus, offers, or new dish announcements on disposable cups.

Movie Promotions
